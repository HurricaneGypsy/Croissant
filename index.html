Killer Croissant - A Tale of Murderous Intent

In this thrilling tale, a seemingly ordinary bakery in a small town becomes the center of a mysterious series of murders. As customers indulge in the delicious croissants, they are unaware of the deadly secret ingredient that transforms these pastries into lethal weapons. The protagonist, a determined detective, must uncover the truth behind the killer croissant before more lives are lost.

And so the story begins....
